Challenges Faced by the Construction Sector

Compared to all other job sectors, construction has the highest number of fatal work injuries. Accidents on construction sites cause thousands of on-the-job injuries and contribute to almost 20 percent of all yearly private industry worker deaths. There are many factors that contribute to construction site injuries and fatalities. The leading causes of worker deaths on construction sites are Falls, Getting struck by an object, Electrocution, Pipefitter Accidents, Equipment failure, etc.
